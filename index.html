COTW Countries of the World

How to play

There are 197 Sovereign Countries on our earth.

Your objective is to name all of the countries within the 15 minute time limit.

Controls

You can start the timer by clicking the and the game will begin. You can end the game early by also clicking on the timer.

Clicking on the remaining countries will show/hide the missing countries on the globe.

Enter a country name in the Name a country field and it will appear on the world map above.
